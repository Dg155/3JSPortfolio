Musical Madness:

  • Project Lead, Producer, Programmer
  • April 2022
  • (Unity, C#)
  • Duration: 7 weeks
  • Team Size: 6

Musical Madness is a game project I co-lead with my roommate the spring quarter of our sophomore year. It is a top-down procedural dungeon crawler/shooter themed around music. I managed a team of 6 along side producing and designing the game myself. I implemented aspects such as the procedural generation, level structure, and the combat system!
