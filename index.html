Hello! I am Daniel Boghossian, a skillful and multidisciplinary college student working on degrees within Computer Science and Game Development. From my earliest memories I remember booting up my old Nintendo DS just to lose myself inside the worlds of Super Mario World and Donkey Kong Country. Since then I knew I wanted to create video games for a living. My goal is to be able to provide children with the same joy and comfort I felt within video games.

During the 8th grade I taught myself my first coding language, sparking my aspiration to create the mechanics and software elements required to breath life into video games. Being able to create something with my own hands, especially something that runs on technology and inculcates our advancements, is an art form that I wish to devote the rest of my life to.

Since that first coding class I created nearly a dozen games, mastering so many new softwares and applications. Everyday I strive to learn something new, to advance my skills and broaden my horizons. I will make an impact on the world.
